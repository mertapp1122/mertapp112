Revolutionary Capabilities

GPT-5 isn't just smarter—it thinks. With built-in reasoning capabilities and unprecedented performance across every domain, it represents the closest we've come to artificial general intelligence.

Advanced Reasoning

GPT-5 includes a built-in routing system that decides when to answer instantly and when to think in steps, delivering profound responses at astonishing speed.

Superior Coding

Achieving 74.9% on SWE-bench Verified and 88% on Aider Polyglot, GPT-5 can create complex web applications and games in minutes, not hours.

Mathematical Excellence

Scoring 94.6% on AIME 2025 without tools, GPT-5 demonstrates PhD-level mathematical reasoning and problem-solving capabilities.

Multimodal Understanding

With 84.2% performance on MMMU, GPT-5 seamlessly processes text, images, and complex visual information with human-level comprehension.

Reduced Hallucinations

45% less likely to contain factual errors than GPT-4o, and when thinking, 80% less likely to hallucinate than OpenAI o3.

Natural Interaction

Less effusively agreeable, fewer unnecessary emojis, more subtle and thoughtful—like chatting with a helpful friend with PhD-level intelligence.

Live Demonstrations

During OpenAI's August 7, 2025 livestream, GPT-5 showcased abilities that would have been unimaginable just months ago.

French Learning App: In just 14 seconds of thinking, GPT-5 generated hundreds of lines of code to create a complete French learning web app with flashcards, quizzes, and interactive games—work that would typically take a software developer several hours.

About OpenAI

Founded in December 2015, OpenAI has evolved from a visionary non-profit into the world's leading artificial intelligence organization, driving the ongoing AI revolution with groundbreaking models like ChatGPT, DALL-E, and now GPT-5.

Mission & Vision

OpenAI's mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI)—AI systems that are generally smarter than humans—benefits all of humanity. Their primary fiduciary duty is to humanity itself.
